The Foundation of My Dreams

Maa, Happy Mother's Day. I know today is supposed to be all about you, and it is, but I can't stand here and talk about my life without talking about both you and Papa together. You both are the only reason I am who I am today. Maa, you are my safest place. You are the quiet, unconditional warmth behind absolutely everything I do. And Papa, you are the backbone. The way you both have silently supported me, giving me the freedom to dream and the safety net I needed when things got tough I don't say it enough, but I notice every single sacrifice.

Sometimes I get so caught up in my own world pushing through my BBA courses, trying to grow the business, or staying up late trying to get the perfect edit for a video. It might look like I'm just chasing my own goals, but the truth is, the drive behind all of that hustle comes entirely from you two. I want to build a future that repays even a fraction of what you've given me. Literally everything I do, I do it for you. Any success I achieve is yours first. There is a beautiful Shayari that perfectly describes how I feel about you both:

"Gher lene ko mujhe jab bhi balayen aayin, Dhal bankar samne maa baap ki duayen aayin. (Whenever troubles came to surround me, the prayers of my parents came forward as a shield.)"

There isn't enough code in the world, or a camera lens wide enough, to capture exactly how much I love you both. Thank you for being the greatest blessing of my life.

Love always, Qusai
